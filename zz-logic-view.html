Dashboard Logic — Business Rules & Definitions

Explains the business rules used in Business Observability dashboards.

Current Definitions

Metric Definition Coverage (App ID) Coverage (Node) Health (App ID) Health (Node) Incidents (Node)
Coverage Percentage of applications from a defined population that have signals being received by the platform. # Apps with signals / # Apps in population # Nodes with signals / # Nodes in population - - -
Health Percentage of signals from an application that are ‘Green’ (i.e. meet defined success criteria). - - # Green signals / # Total signals # Healthy nodes / # Total nodes with signals -
Incidents Count of incidents associated with an application. - - - - Sum of incidents for nodes in App

Worked Example

Example: Home Lending Sub-LOB with sample data.

App ID Total Signals Green Signals App Health %
App-HL-01 5000 4950 99.00%
App-HL-02 12000 11880 99.00%
App-HL-03 (New) 800 720 90.00%
App-HL-04 25000 23750 95.00%
App-HL-05 (Legacy) 1200 960 80.00%

Node Coverage (Current Rule)

(5 apps with signals / 6 apps total) = 83.33%

Node Health (Current Rule, App-Weighted)

((99%+99%+90%+95%+80%) / 5) = 92.60%

Optional Future Rule (Signal-Weighted)

(42260 / 44000) = 96.05%

Current Stage & Roadmap

Metric Current Logic Next Step (Future Maturity)
Coverage Based on manually maintained list of App IDs in Confluence. Automate App ID population from CMDB based on Sub-LOB.
Health Simple average of App Health % (App-Weighted). Signal-weighted health calculation. Introduce business criticality weighting.
Incidents Manual association of incidents to Apps. Automated incident association via tags from ServiceNow.
